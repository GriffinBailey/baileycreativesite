Get Creative in your Fitness!

Welcome to Bailey Creative! We are here to help you design and achieve your health and fitness lifestyle. We can design a personalized fitness regiment to that best fits you. Our facility is equipped with an excellent selection of free-weight equipment, a therapy pool, and a spacious fitness classroom.

We pride ourselves on superior member services. Friendly and knowledgeable staff members are here to help you every step of the way with free introductory instructional sessions, fun and motivating fitness classes, and one-on-one personal training. Amenities include tanning, clean locker rooms and showers, and a pro shop.

Please take some time to get acquainted with us through our website. Feel free to stop in anytime to take a tour of our facility and visit with us about how we can help you on your journey toward wellness and good health.
